About

Fathom Me.

A passionate web developer, a huge believer in technology. My dream involves bringing access to technology to unfortunate people in countries like Myanmar, and making a change.

Born on 16, August, 1997 (19 years old). From Myitkyina, Kachin State, northern part of Myanmar. Moved to Mandalay due to civil war around my home town, in 2012. Then, again was insisted to migrate to Yangon in 2013. Finished high school here and pursued further studies in Singapore. Studied media communications and PR, have vast interest in new media technology, digging further into 'how to make website?', web development and computer science struck my head. Became obsessed with computer programming. Started to involve in web development for serveral start up a few years ago.

Aiming to become better in machine learning and data-mining. I am willingly looking forward to volunteer in AI projects.
